Himalayan Danfe

The Himalayan Monal is beautiful bird, it's also known as the Impeyan phesant.

  • Scientific Name: Monal/ Danfe
  • Average Length: 70 centimeters
  • Habitat: It lives in upper temperate oak-confer forests interespersed with open grassy slopes.
